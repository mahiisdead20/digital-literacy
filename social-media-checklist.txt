# 📱 Social Media Do’s and Don’ts for College Students

## ✅ DO’s
- Maintain a respectful and professional online presence  
- Think before posting any content  
- Use strong passwords to secure your accounts  
- Verify information before sharing it  
- Respect others' privacy and opinions  

---

## ❌ DON’Ts
- Do not share personal or sensitive information publicly  
- Avoid posting offensive or inappropriate content  
- Do not engage in cyberbullying or online arguments  
- Avoid clicking on suspicious links or messages  
- Do not waste excessive time on social media during studies
